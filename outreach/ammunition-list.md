# GEO Audit Outreach Ammunition List (30 Brands)

This list contains 30 highly strategic brand leads categorized into three tiers, with specific reasons why they are optimal targets for cold-outreach GEO audits.

---

## Tier 1: SEO & Marketing Agencies (Highest Priority)
*Why: These agencies have existing client rosters, active SEO/AEO client inquiries, and budget to upsell modern deliverables. They are immediate high-value targets.*

### 1. Siege Media
- **Target URL:** siegemedia.com
- **Why It's a Good Target:** They are a premier content-driven SEO agency. They write high-end content for major SaaS brands. Pitching them how their clients' content is (or isn't) referenced by AI will instantly make them look like cutting-edge experts to their clients.
- **Outreach Angle:** "Your clients' expensive blog posts are ranking #1 on Google, but ChatGPT doesn't cite them. Let's fix that."

### 2. Single Grain (Eric Siu)
- **Target URL:** singlegrain.com
- **Why It's a Good Target:** Founded by Eric Siu, who is extremely vocal on podcasts about AI, marketing, and modern GTM strategies. They love test-driving new marketing technologies.
- **Outreach Angle:** "Eric, I ran your agency brand on Gemini and DeepSeek. Here is where the citation is breaking."

### 3. WEBRIS (Ryan Stewart)
- **Target URL:** webris.org
- **Why It's a Good Target:** Ryan Stewart focuses heavily on systematizing agency deliverables. He is always looking for new high-ticket auditing frameworks to sell to clients.
- **Outreach Angle:** "A new checklist-based auditing service you can add to your client onboarding next week."

### 4. From The Future (FTF)
- **Target URL:** ftf.co
- **Why It's a Good Target:** A highly technical, data-driven SEO agency. They build custom internal tools and love deep technical integration like Schema markup and robots.txt optimizations.
- **Outreach Angle:** "Advanced GEO Schema validation for your enterprise client accounts."

### 5. Grow and Convert
- **Target URL:** growandconvert.com
- **Why It's a Good Target:** A prominent content marketing agency specializing in B2B SaaS. They focus on "pain-point SEO" and high-intent keywords. Showing them which high-intent queries their articles win on AI search is incredibly valuable.
- **Outreach Angle:** "Track whether your pain-point articles actually feed into LLM responses."

### 6. Victorious SEO
- **Target URL:** victoriousseo.com
- **Why It's a Good Target:** A massive mid-market SEO agency that prides itself on structured, campaign-based SEO.
- **Outreach Angle:** "Add AI Citation Share of Voice to your quarterly executive reports."

### 7. Directive Consulting
- **Target URL:** directiveconsulting.com
- **Why It's a Good Target:** The go-to tech/SaaS search marketing agency. They manage massive ad and SEO budgets for enterprise SaaS.
- **Outreach Angle:** "Stop letting competitor brands steal your clients' share of voice on ChatGPT Search."

### 8. Webprofits
- **Target URL:** webprofits.com.au
- **Why It's a Good Target:** A growth-focused digital agency known for early adoption of new channels (previously social, now generative search).

### 9. NP Digital (Neil Patel)
- **Target URL:** npdigital.com
- **Why It's a Good Target:** One of the largest global SEO agencies. If you can get a single director to try this, it opens up hundreds of enterprise client opportunities.

### 10. Coalition Technologies
- **Target URL:** coalitiontechnologies.com
