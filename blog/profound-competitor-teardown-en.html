Three Critical Weaknesses

2. Too complex. Co-mention, Topic Clusters, Mention Position — great for analysts, gibberish to founders.

3. Zero Chinese engines. DeepSeek, Kimi, Doubao, Qwen — covering 2.5B users — completely absent from their platform.
