Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between SEO and GEO?
SEO optimizes for Google rankings. GEO optimizes for AI citations. A site ranking #1 on Google can be completely invisible to ChatGPT — because ChatGPT uses Bing's index, not Google's. Two audiences, two different strategies.
Which AI engines do you check?
ChatGPT, DeepSeek, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, Doubao (ByteDance), Kimi (Moonshot), and Qwen (Alibaba). We're the only GEO monitoring tool covering both Western and Chinese AI engines in a single dashboard.
Why do I need monitoring instead of a one-time scan?
AI engine indexes update continuously. ChatGPT's knowledge base is re-trained periodically; Perplexity and DeepSeek crawl the web in near real-time. A one-time scan tells you your score today — but not whether your optimizations worked, whether a competitor just overtook you, or whether an engine started citing you incorrectly. Weekly monitoring answers all of these.
