FAQ

Questions we get asked.

What's the difference between Free Scan, Deep Report, and Monitor?
Free Scan checks 1 engine (DeepSeek) — tells you if your brand is mentioned at all. No credit card. Deep Report ($20) covers all 4 engines with full citation matrix, competitor analysis, sentiment audit, and action plan — 12-section PDF. Monitor ($200/mo) re-audits weekly, alerts you when citations appear or disappear, and tracks competitor movement over time.
What is GEO and why does it matter?
Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of ensuring your brand appears in AI-generated answers. When a user asks Doubao "best CRM for small business" and your brand isn't mentioned — that's a GEO gap. With 1.2 billion users across Chinese AI engines, being invisible in AI answers is the new equivalent of not ranking on Google.
Why $20 and not $500/month?
Most AEO platforms charge agency retainers because they're built for agencies. We built GEO Audit for brands that just need to know. Start with the free scan. When you need the full picture, Deep Report is $20 one-time. If AI visibility is critical to your business, Monitor keeps you continuously informed at $200/mo.
Why only Chinese engines? What about ChatGPT and Gemini?
Western engines are well-covered by existing tools (SurgeGraph, Profound, Peec). Chinese engines are ignored — yet they serve 1.2 billion users. We focus exclusively on the gap no one else covers. If you need Western engine audits too, we recommend pairing GEO Audit with one of those tools.
How do you actually query the engines?
We query each engine with your brand name and a standardized set of industry-relevant prompts. Every query is executed fresh — we don't cache results. We capture the full response, extract citation data, classify sentiment, and compare against competitor brands in the same query set.
Is this different from SEO?
Yes. SEO optimizes for traditional search engine rankings (Baidu, Google). GEO optimizes for AI-generated answers. A page can rank #1 on Google and still never be cited by an AI engine. GEO Audit bridges that gap by showing you what AI engines actually say about your brand.
Can I re-run the audit later?
Yes. Buy Deep Report again whenever you want a fresh snapshot — many customers run it quarterly. If you need ongoing monitoring (weekly), Monitor does it automatically with change alerts and trend reports.
What if my brand isn't mentioned at all?
That's the most common result — and the most valuable one. 82% of brands we audit have zero citations on at least 2 engines. The report gives you a baseline and a prioritized action plan to change that. You can't fix what you haven't measured.
Do you support non-Chinese brands?
Yes. Many international brands need visibility in Chinese AI engines — for China market entry, for APAC strategy, or because their competitors are already cited. The report is bilingual so your global team can act on it.
How do I pay?
Gumroad handles payment — credit card, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay. Free Scan requires no payment. Deep Report is one-time $20. Monitor is $200/month, cancel anytime.
